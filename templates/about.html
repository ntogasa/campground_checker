What is this?

Campground Scavenger is an app that makes finding and reserving campgrounds easier.

How does it do that?

If you've ever reserved a campground online before, chances are you have used recreation.gov. It's the online portal to all of the federally managed campgrounds in the United States. Thousands of campgrounds in popular locations like Zion, the Grand Canyon, and Yosemite can only be reserved through recreation.gov.

Unfortunately, recreation.gov is grossly inefficient.

On recreation.gov, campgrounds can only be reserved or checked through their individual webpages. If you are only interested in one specific campground, that's not a problem. But what if you want to see if ANY campgrounds in a park are available? There are ~30 different campgrounds in Sequoia National Forest, so to check them all would mean you have to visit ~30 different webpages.

Campground Scavenger can Mass Check

Campground Scavenger allows you to mass check as many campgrounds as you wish with the single click of a button. It also provides links to each campground checked, so you can quickly and easily make reservations. So now you can check all 30 campgrounds in Sequoia National Forest in seconds.

How to use

  1. Go to Campgrounds and search your desired parent zone (i.e. Kings Canyon National Park)
  2. Copy the ID numbers, and go to the Availability page
  3. Paste them in the input field, along with your target dates, and submit
  4. Wait for your results to appear!

I hope this makes it easier for you to plan your next outdoor excursion. Enjoy!


API

You can visit the API to access our campground database. Each campground object in the database includes a name, ID number, and parent zone name.
